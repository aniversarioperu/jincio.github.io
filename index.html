Biography

José Incio is a Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Pittsburgh. His research interest include subnational politics, democracy, and political methodology.

José Incio es candidato a doctor en Ciencia Política de la University of Pittsburgh, sus áreas de interés incluyen política subnacional, democracia, y metodología.
